Red

Used on masks that indicate a positive character. Red can also mean prosperity, loyalty, courage and heroism.

Yellow

Tells the audience that the character is cruel. Yellow can also mean evil, hypocritical, ambitious or sly.

White

Used on masks that indicate the character is evil and hypocritical.

Black

Means the character is neutral. Black also indi- cates impartiality and integrity.

Green

Shows that the character is violent, impulsive and lacks restraint.

Gold and Silver

Show the audience that the character is a god or demon. It can also indicate a ghost or a spirit. Gold And silver symbolize mystery.

Pink

Pink indicates that the character is old and weak. Pink face can give the audience a feeling that the character has a pale face.

Blue

Blue is an indication of neutrality. In addition, Blue can show stubbornness, astuteness and fierceness.
